# CM1 Power Tree & Sequencing

Source: `ArmSoM-CM1-1V1_Sch.pdf` **sheets 2 (Power Diagram), 15 (Power-Discrete),
3 (SOC POWER/GND)**, rev V1.1.

## Input

- **VCC12V_DCIN** — 12 V from adapter (also reaches J8001.40). Backlight/boost
  domain.
- **VCC5V0_SYS** — 5 V system rail (from a DC-DC buck off 12 V, or TypeC 5 V).
  Root of most on-module regulators. Exposed on J8001.34.

## Rails (default voltages)

| Rail | Default | Source | Powers |
|------|--------:|--------|--------|
| **VCC_3V3** | 3.3 V | DC-DC buck off VCC5V0_SYS | SoC VCCIO, WiFi, eMMC/flash, general 3V3 |
| **VCC_1V8** | 1.8 V | DC-DC buck / LDO | SoC IO 1.8 V domains, display IOVCC |
| **VCC_DDR** | 1.36 V (DDR3L) / 1.5 V (DDR3) / 1.8 V (DDR2) | DC-DC buck | DDR memory + SoC DDR PHY |
| **VDD_CPU** | 0.954 V (range 0.754–1.099 V) | PWM-controlled DC-DC buck | A7 CPU core |
| **VDD_0V9** | 0.9 V (≈0.91 V) | LDO | SoC logic / PLL / analog 0.9 V |
| **VCC_PA** | — | from VCC5V0_SYS | speaker amplifier |
| **VCC_RTC / VBAT_RTC** | — | coin cell on J8002.37 | RTC keep-alive |

VDD_CPU is generated by a buck whose FB is steered by **PWM0_CH0_CPU** (a
SoC PWM): `Vout = 1.099 − (1.099 − 0.754) × Duty`, FB = 0.6 V. This is the
DVFS control for the CPU core.

VCC_DDR DDR-type select (sheet 15): DDR2 = 1.81 V (200 K), DDR3L = 1.36 V
(150 K), DDR3 = 1.50 V (127 K) — set by feedback resistor. The fitted module
uses **DDR3L @ 1.36 V**.

## Power-on sequence (sheet 2)

The on-module PMIC / discrete regulators bring rails up in slots:

1. **Slot 1**: VDD_0V9 (0.9 V) and VDD_CPU come up first (DVDD/PMU of SoC).
2. **Slot 2 / 2a**: VCC_DDR (DDR), then VCC_1V8 / flash VCCIO.
3. **Slot 3**: VCC_3V3 (VCCIO, USB, PMUIO).
4. Finally, **nPOR RESET is released 10 ms after PMUIO_VCC3V3 is ready.**

EN thresholds for the discrete regulators are Vih ≈ 1.4–1.5 V.

## Reset / control

- **NPOR_L / nPOR** — power-on reset to the SoC (`RESETB`), gated by the PMU
  control path (sheet 4). Released 10 ms after PMUIO good.
- **Reset Key** path and **TSADC_SHUT** (thermal shutdown) control path also
  feed the PMU/reset logic (sheet 4).
- **PWM0_CH0_CPU** — CPU core voltage DVFS (see above).

## Notes

- Keep filter caps close to SoC/DDR power pins (called out on sheets 3, 5, 10).
- "Star" the analog vs digital supplies; no soft-start inside the LDO
  (designer note, sheet 15). Analog PLL/OSC power is isolated (VDD_0V9 analog
  branch, sheet 15).
